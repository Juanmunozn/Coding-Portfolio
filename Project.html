Amazon Prime Study Case

INTRODUCTION

Overview

Amazon Prime Video is a popular streaming service offered by Amazon.com. It provides subscribers with access to a wide range of movies, TV shows, original content, and other video content.

Why?

An intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface is crucial for a movie streaming service as it directly impacts the overall user experience and, consequently, the success and satisfaction of its subscribers. While integrating artificial intelligence (AI) into movie streaming services can bring many benefits in enhancing user experience, content discovery, and overall industry efficiency.

Initial Assumptions

  • There might be a perceived lack of personalized recommendations or difficulty in tailoring content suggestions based on individual preferences.

  • AI can enhance user experience research by analyzing viewing patterns, preferences, and interactions within Amazon Prime Video, providing insights into user behaviour.

  • Intuitive and easy-to-navigate platforms that provide a seamless experience across devices. Quick access to recently watched, favourite shows, and recommendations.

RESEARCH

Insights

  • Implementing an AI-powered search tool so users can find personalized content suggestions based on their viewing history, preferences, and IMDb ratings.

  • By adding a human touch to the recommendations and considering the broader context of the user's situation, the platform can offer more relevant and enjoyable movie suggestions.

  • User feedback and reviews have highlighted several challenges that impact content discovery and overall satisfaction.

  • Users are more likely to engage with the platform if they can easily explore genres, trending movies, and curated lists.

Interviews Highlights

  • AI-driven algorithms in content search can greatly enhance the overall user experience. Can lead to more accurate recommendations, saving time and increasing user satisfaction.

  • The additional benefits that come with my Amazon Prime Video add significant value to my overall experience.

  • Making easier for users to discover content tailored to their preferences is a must.

  • Confused on which content does my streaming service includes, bugged of paying extra.

  • More accurate recommendations, saving time searching content increases the user satisfaction.

Key Findings

  • Users want a seamless and intuitive experience when searching for and watching movies.

  • Enhancing the search functionality to provide faster and more efficient search results, enabling users to find content seamless.

  • Participants expressed uncomfortable interaction when searching for new content of their preference.

Principles & Recommendations

Principle #1

Enhance the recommendation algorithms by integrating artificial intelligence.

  • Provide a powerful search feature that allows users to find content quickly and efficiently.

  • Implement filters and sorting options to refine search results.

  • Bring the main categories to the main page and not in a menu, for quick access.

Principle #2

Seamless and consistent user experience across various devices, mobile devices, and web browsers.

  • Maintain consistency in design elements such as color scheme, typography, and button styles across the website or application.

  • Limit the number of menu items to avoid overwhelming users.

  • Ensure your design is responsive to different screen sizes, especially for mobile devices.

CONCLUSION

By re-designing the home page, menu and the integration of the new feature. We empower users to seamlessly discover, personalize, and enjoy their entertainment experience through the strategic integration of artificial intelligence into our movie streaming services.
